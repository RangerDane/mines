Minesweeper

Instructions

Click on any cell to reveal it. Don't worry, your first click will always be safe.

Right-click on any cell to flag it. Use this to mark spaces you are sure contain a mine. This feature is optional, but extremely helpful.

The only way to win is to reveal every square that does not contain a mine.

If you accidentally uncover a mine, you lose. But don't worry, you can click undo as many times as you want.

Minesweeper is a game about collapsing probabilities, and following information to make accurate decisions. Click on a cell to uncover it, but be careful not to bank on luck alone - use the information at your disposal to find the location of every mine, and proceed only with confidence.
