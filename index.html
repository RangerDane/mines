Minesweeper

Instructions

Click on any cell to reveal it. Don't worry, your first click will always be safe.

Right-click on any cell to flag it. Use this to mark spaces you are sure contain a mine. This feature is optional, but extremely helpful.

To win the game, reveal every square that does not contain a mine.

If you accidentally uncover a mine, you lose. But don't worry, you can click undo as many times as you want.

Minesweeper is a game about collapsing probabilities, and using data to increase decision-making confidence. Be careful not to bank on luck alone - use the information at your disposal to reason about the location of every mine, and proceed when you are confident. Here are some common patterns:

Common Patterns

Revealed squares will contain a number (1-8), indicating the number of mines immediately adjacent to it, by edge or by corner.
